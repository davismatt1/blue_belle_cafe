Blue Belle Cafe

For a premium blend in Island Bay

A coffee oasis in the heart of Island Bay, Blue Belle cafe is tucked away from the CBD ready for you to discover. We create fabulous handmade food, tasty supreme coffee made with organic milk all in a cosy and warm environment. Come in, feel welcome and enjoy the experience.

  • Premium Coffee

    Serving 'Supreme' coffee, our baristas are trained to deliver quality brewed coffee with every cup. We use premium organic dairy milk, along with dairy alternatives; soy, almond, and rice milk.

  • Cafe Interior

    We pride ourselves in offering a cute and cosy space. You can truly relax and feel comfortable while enjoying your experience of premium coffee, delicious food and attentive customer service.

  • Delicious Food

    Our culinary team prepare a wide range of foods from our delictable cabinet offerings, along with fresh and made to order meals. We provide a range of food catering to various dietary requirements.

Please enquire about our event catering services

Our dedicated culinary team specialise in creating custom cakes and meals for many occasions. From weddings, to 21st parties, to even baby showers and corporate events - please visit us for a free consultation for any of your event catering requirements.
